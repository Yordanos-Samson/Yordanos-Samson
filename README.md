I ship production systems — mobile, web, and AI. From government security apps for **INSA** to a full EdTech SaaS at **GlobeDock Academy**, I build things that work in the real world. Currently leading the **TalentDock** stack: headless open-source LMS + Laravel + Next.js, fully self-hosted on a VPS I own end-to-end.

**My approach** — I treat engineering as product leverage. I'd rather ship one system that lasts five years than five demos that ship Monday. I own deployment, observability, and the on-call rotation for everything I build.

**Where I'm headed** — building infrastructure that lasts: AI tools that solve real workflows, mobile-first products that work in low-bandwidth realities, and SaaS systems that scale from a few hundred to a few million users.

</details>

</details>
